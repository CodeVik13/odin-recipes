Bourbon Baked Beans

alt="bourbon-baked-beans"

Description

This dish is going to blow you mind!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Chili Beans
  • Bacon
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Start frying the bacon.
  2. Pour 2dl water and add the tomato sauce into add.
  3. Finally add the chili beans and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
